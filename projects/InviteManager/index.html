Manage Invites & Reward Your Community

A powerful Discord bot that tracks invites and manages role-based rewards, helping you incentivize community growth and recognize your most active members.

Key Features

Invite Tracking

Keep track of each member's total invites with detailed statistics.

Role-Based Rewards

Create, delete, and view invite-based role rewards to incentivize community growth.

Join Notifications

Announce new members and their inviters in your server's system channel.

Welcome New Members

The bot automatically sends a notification to your system channel when a new member joins, showing who invited them to help you track community growth.

  • Customizable welcome messages

Simple Commands

Track Invites

The /invite command allows users to check their current number of invites or view another user's invites.

/invite - View your own invites
/invite @username - View another user's invites

Create Rewards

The /reward create command sets up a new invite-based role reward.

/reward create @role [invites] - Create a new role reward

List Rewards

The /reward list command shows all active invite-based role rewards.

/reward list - View all active rewards

Delete Rewards

The /reward delete command removes an invite-based role reward.

/reward delete @role - Delete a role reward
